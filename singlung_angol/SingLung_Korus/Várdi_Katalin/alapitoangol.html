Katalin Várdi
Pulmonologist, somnologist,respiratory rehabilitation and palliative care specialist.

Started to work at Dept. of Pulmonology, Semmelweis Medical University. She was trained 1991-1995 at the Churchill Hospital, Oxford UK. For more than 10 years she headed the Dept. of Respiratory disease, Respiratory Rehabilitation and Chronic conditions at Törökbálint County Hospital.
She is co-founder of „SingLungChoir ''.

György Philipp, conductor, musician and artist and Katalin elaborated „SingLung method” which is a specialised art project of vocal physiotherapy and common creative work amongst patients and professional artists, patients' teachers and fellow team members, ending in a joint performance for public audience.

(She shares the idea that singing not only helps improve breath control, and engages all the right muscles for better breathing, in combination with physical, breathing and vocal exercises. but the teamwork with professional artists gives extra social and psychological benefits on top for both the patients and the healthy caregivers.)

Katalin now works as freelancer respiratory physician and runs Sleep Lab at Farkasgyepű.
She was born and raised in Budapest, 57 years old. Has 4 children and a little grandson.
