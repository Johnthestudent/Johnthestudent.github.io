SingLung

Music connects us.
There are no boundaries in music.
Music is for everyone.
Singing is joy, singing heals.
If it's difficult, sing or buzz, even to yourself, because it takes you away from the
problem and you solve everything on your own.
Sing when you're in a good mood, because it brings joy to others.
On weekdays, remember that air flows and finds the right way,
just like you learned during the vocal-warm-up, you just have to practice.
