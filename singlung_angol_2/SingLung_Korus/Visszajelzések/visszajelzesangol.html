Feedback

The two concerts were really an uplifting feeling. Not only the audience of the concert, but also the members of the BFZ gave heartily bravo during our parade on Saturday, but the Sunday's concert turned out much better!
What's more, after the Saturday night concert, at the backstage several people came with tears in their eyes and told that we shamed them. Our will to live gives them strength. Iván Fisher asked our choir for our presentation before the BFZ concert because he supports the SingLung vocal physiotherapy developed by pulmonologist, Dr. Katalin Várdi and our conductor, György Philipp.
This therapy improves the quality of lung patients’ life with a wide variety of recreational opportunities. The dedication of musicians - György Philipp, Orsolya Ambrus, Eszter Balogh, Lóránt Najbauer - as music therapists is fantastic! The Buddhist Mr. Sipos not only prepared and transformed everyone not for the concert but also for their future life by helping to find the buzz and inner voice!
It is a special pleasure that on Saturday the master sent our performance to his guru in India who enjoyed the Constructive Meeting of East and West! The two concerts were much more and deeper than healing and breathing improvement. Improving the quality of life and healing have gained a new meaning in the area of spiritual deepening. Everyone started a spontaneous transformation or arrived there after the one-week course.

Today, my mother performed with the choir as a guest for the invitation of conductor, Iván Fischer of the Festival orchestra at Müpa.
This is such a sentence that is a miracle to describe, since my mother, with her heavily damaged and sick lungs, is good if she doesn't get sick during a long walk.
But my mom and many other patients sang on stage today in such a way that everyone burst into tears and applauded. I can't tell you how proud we are of her and how grateful we are to those who helped them!

After the performance, my singer roommates and me were so excited that we couldn't fall asleep until 12:15 a.m. Thank you very much.
Thank you very much for everything, we had a great time and we are glad that we could be a part of this beautiful event.
