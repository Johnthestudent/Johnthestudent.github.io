György Philipp conductor, director, „Lábán Rudolf” awarded composer.
He gained his degree in conducting at Liszt Ferenc Music Academy in 2005.
He directed, conducted numerous theatre performances.
Now he is leading art teacher at AKG High School, Budapest and conducting the SingLung Choir.
Father of three fantastic kids.
He considers that his mission is to educate generations with devotion to music, as well as spreading the idea of active daily singing or music playing.
